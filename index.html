Adventure

I love adventure in endless forms. I love adventure that's not the norm, I love adventure in a storm. I love it here and I love it there, I love adventures everywhere. For as long as I can remember I've loved to see whats around the trail's bend and its lead me to the most joyful and memorable experiences in my limited time here on Earth. My dad also read green eggs and ham to me as a kid.

Pacific Crest Trail 2021

My formative adventure. The best summer of my life as I spent 4 months walking a dusty path through Calfornia, Oregon and Washington. Through smoke and heat, frost and pain, I realized that not only can you be supremely happy in spite of being dirty, tired, and hungry, you might find yourself happy because of those things.

Pfiffner Traverse July 2023

My long-planned, very first high route from Rocky Mountain National Park to I-70. An amazing 5-day solo adventure full of wildflowers, navigation skill building and thunder- and hail-based humblings. Probably the greatest bang-for-my-buck adventure I have ever done.
